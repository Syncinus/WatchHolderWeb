Float™ Technology

All watch holders from watchholder.ca float, with or without a watch. All our watch holders are tested extensively to ensure that your watch is kept save, no matter what happens. Even if the watch holder is knocked off of your boat, it will keep your watch safe.

3D Printed

All the watchholder.ca watch holders are 3D printed in house. They are printed in high quality PLA, making sure they are long lasting and watertight. 3D printing allows the watch holders to be incredibly bouyant, and to have complex shapes like the Watch Holder 5.
